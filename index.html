Country’s first Robotics and IoT Training Institute launched in Thimphu

On 1 July, Bhutan’s first Robotics and IoT training institute (RITTI) was launched. In a country where dreams of becoming a doctor or an officer have long dominated young people’s aspirations, RITTI offers a chance for youth to dream of other exciting professions in the world of robotics. The youth can learn the fundamentals of robotics and the Internet of Things (IoT), preparing them for a rapidly evolving technological landscape.

's FAQs

What are the fees for the classes in RITI?

The fees for regular classes are Nu.2950, Nu.2750, Nu.3950 and Nu.5000 for Regular, Summer, Winter and Python classes, respectively.

How long are the duration of the classes in RITI?

The regular classes lasts around 8 months, and the winter, summer and python classes all lasts about a month.

Is there perhaps an age limit for the application to the classes?

No, the teaching and learning is adaptable for all ages and there are no barriers barring the people from the learning.

How is the refund policy in RITI?

For the regular, summer and winter classes, the learners get a trial period for 3 days. If the learner doesn't want to continue taking the classes, they are refunded the original sum paid after deducting Nu.500.
